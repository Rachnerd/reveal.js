Waterfall

  • Old method proven to be inefficient
  • Every phase of the project is executed in series

Agile

  • Work in iterations
  • Requirements and solutions evolve during the project
  • Lots of communication with end user and stakeholders

Sprints

  • Each sprint has a goal and lasts around 2 or 3 weeks
  • Teams have a velocity (average points per sprint)
  • Changes during sprint can be re-negotiated between PO and development team

Rituals

  • Sessions during a sprint
  • Preparation to reflection

Daily standup

  • Describe yesterday's goal status
  • Mention potential impediments
  • Explain today's goal

Poker sessions

  • Discuss high-over solution
  • Estimate the amount of work
  • Check DoR (definition of ready)

Retrospectives

  • Reflect on the previous sprint
  • Find improvements for next sprint
  • Discuss incidents during the previous sprint

Sprint reviews

  • Demo delivered business value to stakeholders
  • Interact with people outside of the team
  • Explain the next sprint goal
